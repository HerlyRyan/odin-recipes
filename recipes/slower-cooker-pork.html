Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Recipe

Root beer and pulled pork might not seem like a likely pairing but here it makes all the difference. Topped with your favorite BBQ sauce, it's sure to bring rave reviews.

Ingredients

The Allrecipes community adores this Slow Cooker Pulled Pork recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Pork Tenderloin: 1 (2 pound) pork tenderloin
  • Beer: 1 (12 fluid ounce) can or bottle root beer
  • Barbeque Sauce: Y1 (18 ounce) bottle your favorite barbecue sauce
  • Hamburger Buns: 8 hamburger buns, split and lightly toasted

How to Make Slow Cooker Pulled Pork Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade Slow Cooker Pulled Pork:

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Place pork tenderloin in a slow cooker; pour root beer over top.
  3. Cover and cook on Low until pork shreds easily, 6 to 7 hours. Note: the actual length of time may vary according to the individual slow cooker.
  4. Drain well. Stir in barbecue sauce.
  5. Serve on hamburger buns.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
