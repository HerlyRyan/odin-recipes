Warm Berry Compote Recipe

This berry compote is made in a slow cooker with frozen berries and orange juice. It's a yummy dessert by itself or over ice cream.

Ingredients

The Allrecipes community adores this Warm Berry Compote recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • Berries: 6 cups frozen mixed berries
  • Sugar: ½ cup white sugar
  • Orange Juice: ¼ cup orange juice
  • Orange Zest: 1 ½ teaspoons finely grated orange zest
  • Cornstarch: 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Water: 2 tablespoons water

How to Make Warm Berry Compote Step-By-Step

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade Warm Berry Compote:

  1. Stir frozen berries, sugar, orange juice, and orange zest together in a slow cooker. Cover and cook on High until bubbling, about 1 1/2 hours.
  2. Stir cornstarch and water together in a cup until fully dissolved. Stir into berry mixture. Cover again and cook until thickened, 5 to 10 minutes. Serve warm or at room temperature.
